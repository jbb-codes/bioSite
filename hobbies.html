Here are some photos and more information about Dom's hobbies. Dom started his mixed martial arts hobby in 2020 when regular gyms were closed down. Dom has also been studying Japanese for two and a half years now and has learned two thousand Kanji and six thousand words in the last year. He hopes to one day travel to Japan to learn about the culture and hone his Japanese language studies.
